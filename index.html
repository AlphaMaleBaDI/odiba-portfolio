ProofPass

Live on Stellar Testnet
Zero-Knowledge Age Verification Protocol
A reusable zero-knowledge identity credential system for the Stellar network. Users prove they are at least 18 without revealing their actual age. Circom zk-SNARK proofs are generated entirely in-browser via snarkjs WASM, verified on-chain by a Soroban smart contract using native BLS12-381 pairing checks, and issued as soulbound on-chain credentials. Other Web3 apps can instantly query credential status without re-verification. Built for the Stellar Hacks: Real-World ZK hackathon.
